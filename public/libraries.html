About Our Library Partnerships

Book Club Event Finder works by aggregating publicly available event data from library RSS feeds and event calendars. We update our database daily to ensure readers have access to the most current book club events, reading groups, and literary discussions.

All event registration and attendance is managed by the individual libraries. When you click to register for an event, you'll be directed to the library's official registration page. We simply make it easier to discover these wonderful community programs in one central location.

Are you a library? If your library offers book club programs and you'd like to be included in our aggregator, or if you have questions about how we use your event data, please reach out to us.
